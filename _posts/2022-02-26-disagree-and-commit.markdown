# Advantages / Strengths / Benefits

* Helps with Decision Paralysis
  * https://en.wikipedia.org/wiki/Analysis_paralysis
* Shortens SDLC timelines by completing planning phase on time
* Can anbel those with most mastery/involvement to move forward
* Can work to establish trust in team
* Generally supports the majority

# Disadvantages / Weaknesses / Drawbacks

* Those on the disagree side may be unhappy
* Is not always a truly democratic approach

# How to Succeed

* Timebox RFCs
* Arch Review Board
* Rely on those with subject mastery
* Accept failure as an option and not an inherently negative outcome
    * We learn from our mistakes
    * Fail fast
